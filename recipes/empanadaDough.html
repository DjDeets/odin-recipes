Empanada Dough

Description

This is a basic recipe for Empanada dough. It consists of 4 ingredients (flour, water, a stick of butter, and an egg). This recipe is classically inspired.

Empanadas are a great way of using leftovers. Use this recipe to make a dough which you can then stuff all your leftovers. Empanadas are a great way to make a meal while making food last longer.

Ingredients

  • 370g Flour
  • 80-120 mL Water
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Chop up the stick of butter into smaller chunks
  2. Combine the flour and chunks of butter together
  3. Crack in the egg and continue to combine
  4. Gradually add water while combining until the ingredients form a ball of dough
  5. Knead the heck out of that dough

Your dough is now ready! You can now use it to prepare empanadas by breaking off smaller balls of dough, rolling them flat (1 mm), placing filling in the center, and wrapping the dough around the filling. If the dough starts to dry, you can use egg wash or water to help form the empanada. You then proceed to cook the empanada in the oven (200 degrees for 10 minutes or until looks done).
